# Onboarding Images

Save the provided images to this folder with these exact names:

1. **team-meeting.jpg** - Team at meeting table (Image 1) - for Welcome step
2. **professional.jpg** - Professional woman portrait (Image 5) - for "What's your superpower?" step
3. **handshake.jpg** - Business handshake (Image 2) - for "What gets you excited?" step
4. **collaboration.jpg** - Team collaboration (Image 4) - for "How do you work?" step
5. **tech-work.jpg** - Woman with tech overlay (Image 3) - for "Our Promise" step

## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG
- Recommended size: 1024x1024 or similar square aspect ratio
- The app will crop them to remove any text overlays automatically via the image positioning

## Notes:
- Images will be displayed in rounded rectangles with a subtle teal overlay
- White border and shadow for depth
- Images are responsive and will scale based on screen size
